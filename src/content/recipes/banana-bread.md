## Ingredients

- 3 very ripe bananas (mashed)
- 3 eggs
- 3 ½ tablespoons (52g) plain Greek yogurt or 57g butter or applesauce
- 168g almond flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ¾ teaspoon baking powder
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp apple cider vinegar to activate baking soda
- add ins: walnuts, blueberries …
- to make it a chocolate cake add cacao powder an chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 175°
2. **Mash bananas** in a medium bowl. Whisk in eggs, Greek yogurt, and vanilla extract.
3. **In another bowl,** mix almond flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
4. **Gently fold** the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined.
5. **Pour batter** into a loaf pan
6. **Bake for 35-45 minutes** (loaf) or 18-22 minutes (muffins), until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
7. **Cool for 10 minutes**, then enjoy!
