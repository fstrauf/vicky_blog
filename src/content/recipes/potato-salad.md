## Ingredients

- 900 gr Potatoes
- 240 gr Greek yogurt
- 30 gr Dijon mustard
- Fresh dill - 30 milliliters, chopped
- Fresh parsley - 30 milliliters, chopped
- 60 gr Red onion, finely chopped
- 120 gr Pickled cucumbers, finely chopped
- Salt & pepper
- 15 ml Apple cider vinegar

## 
Instructions

1. Boil the cubed potatoes in a large pot until tender, then drain and let them cool.
2. In a large bowl, mix the Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, chopped dill, parsley, salt, and pepper. Add apple cider vinegar if desired.
3. Add the cooled potatoes to the bowl along with the finely chopped red onion and pickled cucumbers.
Gently mix until the potatoes are evenly coated with the dressing.
4. Refrigerate the salad for at least an hour to allow flavors to meld.
5. Before serving, give the salad a quick stir and garnish with a sprinkle of paprika or chives, if desired.
