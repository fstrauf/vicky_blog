## Ingredients

- 200g mixed nuts (chopped)
- 65g mixed seeds (sunflower, pumpkin, chia, etc.)
- 65g shredded coconut
- 3 large eggs
- 100g peanut butter
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp vanilla extract
- 3-4 tbsp coconut oil or melted butter
- 2 tbsp Milk
- 1-2 chcocolate
- 
- optional: 1-2 tbsp honey

## Instrucitons

1. Preheat oven to 160°C (325°F). Line a baking dish with parchment paper.
2. Mix dry ingredients (nuts, seeds, coconut, salt, and cinnamon/cocoa) in a bowl.
3. Whisk eggs and stir in peanut butter + vanilla until smooth.
4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, mixing until well coated.
5. Press mixture firmly into the lined dish (1-2 cm thick).
6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden brown and set.
7. Cool completely, then cut into bars.
