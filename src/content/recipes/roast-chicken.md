### **Ingredients**


### Chicken & Veggies

- 1 whole chicken
- 2 medium potatoes
- 2 sweet potatoes
- 4 carrot
- 3 beetroots
- 2 onion, quartered
- 3 garlic cloves, smashed
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 3 rosemary sprigs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp dried thyme or rosemary
- 1/2 cup chicken broth or water (optional)

### Marinade

- 100 g / 1 stick unsalted butter _, melted_
- juice of 1 lemon, _keep lemon wedges to stuff into chicken_
- 4 garlic cloves _, paste_
- 2 tsp fresh or dried rosemary _, finely chopped (Note 1)_
- 1 tbsp parsley _, finely chopped (Note 1)_
- 1/2 tsp each salt and black pepper

### **Instructions**:

1. **Preheat Oven**: Preheat to 200°C (400°F).
2. **Prepare Chicken**: Pat the chicken dry. Rub it all over (including under the skin if possible) with 2 tablespoons olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, and thyme or rosemary. Place the lemon halves and garlic inside the cavity.
3. **Prepare Vegetables**: Toss potatoes, carrots, and onion with the remaining olive oil, salt, and pepper. Arrange them in a roasting pan.
4. **Roast**: Place the chicken on top of the vegetables. Pour chicken broth into the pan for extra moisture (optional).
5. **Cook**: Roast in the oven for 1 hour 15 minutes to 1 hour 30 minutes (or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F / 75°C in the thickest part of the chicken). Baste with the juices occasionally.
6. **Rest and Serve**: Let the chicken rest for 10–15 minutes before carving. Serve with the roasted vegetables.
