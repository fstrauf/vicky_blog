- 1 cup **90g shredded/desiccated coconut**
- ⅓ cup **80ml coconut milk**
- 15 **Medjool dates**
- 180 g **Dark chocolate**
1. Mix coconut with coconut milk
2. pit dates and fill with the coconut mixture
3. melt chocolate and dip date
4. place in the fridge at least 30 minutes before serving
