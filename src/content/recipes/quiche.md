## Ingredients


### Filling

- 200 g Spinach or leek
- 250 g Bacon (or more)
- 4 eggs
- 3 yellow onions
- 120 ml heavy cream
- 120 ml milk
- 100 g grated cheese
- feta
- 1/4 tsp salt & pepper

### Pastry

- 150 g cold butter, cubed
- 200 g wholemeal flour
- pinch salt
- 1 egg
- 2-3 tsp of cold water

***roll out dough after chilling and place it in the form, put in the freezer for 30 mins, then blind bake


## Instructions


### Pastry

1. Mix flour and salt in a large bowl and add cubed butter.
2. Rub butter and flour together between the palms of your hands until it becomes like sand.
3. Make a hole in the middle and add 1 egg, mix the ingredients and add the cold water.
4. Form a ball, wrap it and chill for at least 30 mins
5. Take out the dough and roll it out to roughly 3mm and place it in the form, put it in the freezer for 20-30 mins and preheat the oven to 200 C
6. prickle dough with a fork and put weights in (rice on parchment paper)
7. bake for 15-18 mins
8. Lift out parchment/weights. Bake **5–10 minutes** more until the bottom looks dry and lightly golden.

### Filling

1. cut the bacon and roast it in the pan until crisp
2. half the onions and cut them into stripes, put in the pan to caramelise
3. wash the spinach and sauté in the same pan, you might have to use a bit of olive oil
4. mix eggs with cream & milk and add all the other ingredients
5. pour on the dough and bake for 45-50 mins at 180 C
