**Chocolate Banana Egg Pancakes** recipe for you:


### **Ingredients:**

- 1 ripe banana
- 2 eggs
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
- 1/2 tsp baking powder (for fluffier pancakes)
- A pinch of salt
- Butter or oil for cooking

### **Instructions:**

1. Mash the banana in a bowl until smooth.
2. Add the eggs and whisk until well combined.
3. Mix in the cocoa powder, vanilla extract, baking powder, and salt.
4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.
5. Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan to form pancakes.
6. Cook for about 1-2 minutes on one side, then flip and cook for another minute.
7. Serve warm with honey, syrup, or extra banana slices!
